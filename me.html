This is me

After studying mechanical engineering for a year and making some of the closest friends but not really finding my passion I changed to studying "media and computer science" at Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität München.

That's where I felt more at home although it took me a while to figure out which of the vast possibilities you have as a person with a software engineering background I wanted to choose for my future. Through courses such as interaction design and human factors I found that UI/UX design is what enthuses me most. Combining creativity with technical skills and the goal to design useful intuitive products to facilitate everyday life and help people with it is what I want to do - what makes me happy.

Another thing that makes me happy is being outside in the nature, get inspired by acknowledging the seemingly simple, given things in life. Taking a walk through Englischer Garten in Munich, hiking through the beautiful mountains in the Bavarian Alps, cycling through fields and hills with no destination is what brings me back down to earth.
